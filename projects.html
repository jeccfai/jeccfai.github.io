Skin Lesion Analysis
This project is the final year project I am currently carrying out for my penultimate year of university. The project is research based and is about the computational analysis of pigmented skin lesions such as skin cancers (e.g. melanoma, non-melanoma) using Computer Vision. My motive in this project is to compare deep learning methods to traditional methods.
Artificial Neural Network
In my 2nd year of university, one of coursework tasks was to develop an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) using the error backpropogation algorithm. This ANN was used to predict the pan evaporation given predictors such as temperature, wind speed, humidity and more. The project included pre-processing of the data in which I used Weka to do so. Randomising, splitting and normalising my data before passing it into the ANN.

Helpdesk Website
During the 2nd year of university, the entire course was split into teams and given the task to develop a helpdesk webiste. This module was split over two semesters, the first was to develop with a team of your choice, a helpdesk website from scratch. For this semester, our team made use of HTML, CSS, PHP Laravel, phpMyAdmin, SQL and tools such as Slack to assist us in communication. I was involved in the design of wireframes and putting together a design report for the website. I also had a hand in developing using PHP Laravel which was a new experience for me.

The second semester involved a similar task however the teams were randomised and we had to develop the website using a CMS of the choices, WordPress, Joomla or Drupal. This part of the module I was in charge of implementing and designing the website, finding plugins I required along the way. I also lead the demonstration of this website, taking on any questions when asked. This semester, our team used WhatsApp for communication and Trello to keep on track.
